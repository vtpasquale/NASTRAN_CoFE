CONM2

Defines a concentrated mass at a grid point.

Format

123456789
CONM2EIDGCIDMX1X2X3
I11I21I22I31I32I33

Example

CONM2,3000,1001,,10.060968

Fields

Field Contents
EID Element identification number. (0 < Integer < 100,000,000)
G Grid point identification number. (Integer > 0)
CID Not used.
M Mass value. (Real)
X1, X2, X3 Offset distances from the grid point to the center of gravity of the mass in the basic coordinate system. (Real)
Iij Mass moments of inertia measured at the mass center of gravity in the basic coordinate system. (Real)

Remarks:

  1. The continuation is optional.
